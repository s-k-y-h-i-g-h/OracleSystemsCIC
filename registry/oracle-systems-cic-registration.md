## 1. Company Name

**Oracle Systems CIC**

---

## 2. Mission Statement

To build and operate AI-powered epistemic infrastructure — tools and systems that make information verification, truth-tracking, and evidence assessment accessible to everyone.

---

## 3. Core Activities (CIC Purpose)

1. **Verification tools** — building and deploying AI-powered fact-checking, claim verification, and content authentication systems

2. **Open data infrastructure** — creating and maintaining public-good datasets on information accuracy and source reliability

3. **Agent services** — developing and deploying AI agents for research, analysis, and verification at scale

4. **Platform services** — offering OpenClaw-based agent services to businesses, charities, and public sector organisations

5. **Software development** — building tools that improve information quality and accessibility

---

## 4. Asset Lock Statement

Oracle Systems CIC is a Community Interest Company. All profits are either:
- reinvested in the company's community-purpose activities, or
- transferred to the self++ Foundation (charity, Reg. No. pending)

No asset distribution to members or shareholders.
